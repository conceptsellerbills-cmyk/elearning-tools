# Best Corporate Training Platforms in 2025: Employee Learning and Development Solutions

Companies that invest in employee development see 24% higher profit margins and 218% higher revenue per employee. ## What Corporate Training Platforms Must Have

- **SCORM/xAPI support:** Import existing training content
- **Compliance tracking:** Mandatory training completion reporting
- **Blended learning:** Self-paced + live virtual instructor-led training
- **Integrations:** HRIS, Slack, Teams, Zoom
- **Reporting:** Completion rates, assessment scores, time-on-learning
- **Mobile:** Training accessible on phones and tablets

## FAQ

### What LMS do most companies use?

Cornerstone OnDemand and Workday Learning dominate large enterprises. TalentLMS and iSpring Learn are most popular with SMBs. Docebo is the fastest-growing platform in the mid-market.

### What is SCORM and why does it matter?

SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model) is the standard format for e-learning content. SCORM compliance means your courses track completion, scores, and time-on-content in any compatible LMS — essential for compliance training.

### How much does a corporate LMS cost?

SMB LMS platforms range from $2-8/user/month. Enterprise platforms are typically $20,000-$100,000+/year depending on users and features. Open-source options (Moodle) are free to host but require technical maintenance.
